## Prayer & Renunciation

Deacon: Let us pray to the Lord.

People/Choir: Lord, have mercy.

Priest: O Lord, Master, He who is, thou has created man in thine own image and likeness and has given him the authority of everlasting life. Then, when he fell away through sin, thou did not abandon him, but has provided for the salvation of the world through the incarnation of thy Christ. Do thou thyself redeem also this thy creature from the bondage of the enemy and receive him/her into thy heavenly kingdom. Open the eyes of his/her understanding that the illumination of thy Gospel may shine brightly in him/her. Couple with his/her life a luminous angel who shall deliver him/her from every plot of the adversary, from encounter with the evil one, from the demon of noonday, and from evil apparitions.

Expel from him/her/them every evil and unclean spirit, which lies hidden and lurks in his/her/their heart.

Expel from him/her/them every evil and unclean spirit, which lies hidden and lurks in his/her/their heart.

Expel from him/her/them every evil and unclean spirit, which lies hidden and lurks in his/her/their heart.

A spirit of deception, a spirit of evil, a spirit of idolatry and all greed; a spirit of falsehood and of all uncleanness which operates according to the teaching of the devil. And make him/her a rational sheep of the holy flock of thy Christ, an honorable member of thy Church, a consecrated vessel, a son of light, and an heir of thy kingdom; that having conducted himself/herself in accordance with thy commandments, and having preserved the seal unbroken, and having kept his/her garment unstained, he/she may receive the blessedness of the saints in thy kingdom.

Through the grace and compassion, and love towards mankind of Thine Only-begotten Son, with whom Thou art blessed, together with Thine all-holy and good and life-creating Spirit: now and ever, and unto ages of ages.
