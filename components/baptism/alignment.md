
## Alignment with Christ & Prayer

Priest: Have you united yourself with Christ?

Candidate/Sponsor: I have united myself.

Priest: Have you united yourself with Christ?

Candidate/Sponsor: I have united myself.

Priest: Have you united yourself with Christ?

Candidate/Sponsor: I have united myself.

Priest: Bow down and worship Him.

Candidate/Sponsor: I bow down before the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Spirit: the Trinity, One in essence and inseparable.

Priest: Blessed is God, Who wills that all men be saved, and come unto the knowledge of the truth: now and ever, and unto ages of ages.

People/Choir: Amen.

Deacon: Let us pray to the Lord.

People/Choir: Lord, have mercy.

Priest: O Master, Lord our God, call thy servant, Name, to thy holy Illumination, and deem him/her worthy of thy great grace of thy holy baptism. Wash away from him/her all that is old, and grant him/her to be born again unto life everlasting; and fill him/her with the power of thy Holy Spirit, unto union with Thy Christ that he/she may be no more a child of the body, but a child of thy kingdom.  

Through the good will and grace of Thine Only-begotten Son, with whom Thou art blessed, together with Thine All-Holy, good, and life-creating Spirit; now and ever, and unto ages of ages. 

People/Choir: Amen.

*Choir repeats the Theophany Hymn as the candidates and sponsors move to the front of the Church.*
