
## Gospel

Priest: Peace by to all.

People/Choir: And to thy spirit.

Priest: *Announces the Gospel reading.*

People/Choir: Glory to Thee, O Lord, glory to Thee.

Priest: *Intones the appointed Gospel reading.*

People/Choir: Glory to Thee, O Lord, glory to Thee.

*It is customary for a homily to be given at this time.*
