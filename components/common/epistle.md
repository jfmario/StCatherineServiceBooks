## Prokeimenon and Epistle

Priest/Deacon: Let us attend!

Reader: *Chants the refrain of the prokeimenon.*

People/Choir: *Repeat the refrain.*

Reader: *Chants other verses of the prokeimenon.*

People/Choir: *Repeat the refrain.*

Reader: *Chants the first part of the refrain.*

People/Choir: *Complete the refrain.*

Priest/Deacon: Wisdom!

Reader: *Announces the Epistle reading.*

Priest/Deacon: Let us attend!

Reader: *Intones the appointed Epistle reading.*

Priest: Peace be to thee that readest.
