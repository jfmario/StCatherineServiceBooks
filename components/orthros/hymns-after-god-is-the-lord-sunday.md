
## Hymns

*Sing the appropriate hymns of the day, based on appointed rubrics. The usual pattern follows:*

- The Resurrection Hymn for the Tone of the Week.

Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.

- The Hymn for the Saint of the Day, or a repeat of the above.

Both now and ever, and unto ages of ages. Amen.

- The assigned Theotokion for the tone matching the previous hymn.

*On major festal days, the hymn of the feast is sung 3 times instead.*
