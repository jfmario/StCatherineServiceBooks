
## God is the Lord

*This is usually sung in the tone of the following hymn, if known. Tone 6 is provided here on the following page.*

**Refrain**: God is the Lord and hath appeared unto us. Blessed is He that cometh in the Name of the Lord.

- O give thanks unto the Lord and call upon his Holy Name. *Refrain*
- All nations compassed me about, but in the Name of the Lord will I destroy them. *Refrain*
- This is the Lord's doing; it is marvelous in our eyes. *Refrain*
