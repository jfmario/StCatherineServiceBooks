
## The Praises

*At this point, you may need to switch to the Sunday/Festal Orthros book before possibly switching back.*

- *If there are assigned verses for the Praises, sing them from the Sunday/Festal Orthros book instead of the following 3 Psalms.*
- *If the Great Doxology is appointed, sing that from the Sunday/Festal Orthros book instead of the Little Doxology.*
    - *If a Divine Liturgy is to follow, do so after the Doxology and associated hymns.*
    - *If a Divine Liturgy is NOT to follow, the Epistle and Gospel (as from the Divine Liturgy) are inserted and then the Sunday/Festal Orthros continues.*
- *If the Little Doxology is appointed, return to this book after the Praises.*
