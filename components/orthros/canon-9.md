
## Canon, continued

- Ode 9

## Megalynarion

*Sing the megalynarion of the Theotokos, preferably in the same tone as the canon. The Magnifications refrain from before can serve as the second part of this.*

It is truly meet to bless thee, O Theotokos, the ever-blessed and all-blameless and the Mother of our God.

More honorable than the Cherubim and more glorious beyond compare than the Seraphim, thou who without corruption barest God the Word and art truly Theotokos, we magnify thee.
