## Hymns of the Day

*Sing the appropriate hymns of the day. The usual Saturday pattern follows.*

**Resurrection Hymn for the Tone of the Week**

*Note: There may be a festal between "Glory" and "Both now" Otherwise, "Glory... Both now..." is chanted as one verse. Either way, the theotokion or other hymn follows "Amen."*

Glory to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.

Both now and ever, and unto ages of ages. Amen.
