## Magnification Hymn

Priest: ...  Especially our all-holy, immaculate, most blessed and glorious Lady, the Theotokos and ever-virgin Mary.

*On most Sundays, "It is Truly Meet" (next page) is sung. If the service is the Liturgy of St Basil, "All of Creation Rejoices" (the following page) is sung instead. Sometimes, other hymns are appointed.*
