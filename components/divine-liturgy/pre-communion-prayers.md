
## Pre-Communion Prayers

I believe, O Lord, and I confess that Thou art truly the
Christ, the Son of the living God, Who didst come into
the world to save sinners, of whom I am chief. And I
believe that this is truly Thine own immaculate Body
and that this is truly Thine own precious Blood.

Wherefore, I pray Thee, have mercy upon me and
forgive my transgressions both voluntary and
involuntary, of word and of deed, of knowledge and of
ignorance, and make me worthy to partake without
condemnation of Thine immaculate mysteries
unto remission of sins and unto life everlasting. Amen.

Of Thy mystic supper, O Son of God, accept me today
as a communicant, for I will not speak of Thy mystery
to Thine enemies, neither will I give Thee a kiss as did
Judas, but like the thief will confess Thee: Remember
me, O Lord, in Thy kingdom.

Not unto judgment, nor unto condemnation be my
partaking of Thy holy mysteries, O Lord, but unto the
healing of soul and body.
